GAT — General Aptitude Test

The GAT is a single common test for all CUET candidates regardless of stream — same paper for Commerce, Science and Humanities.

One test for everyone. The CUET GAT (General Test) is identical for all students — Commerce, Science or Humanities. It covers General Knowledge, Current Affairs, Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, and English Language.
